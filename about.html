About Textsura

Our Story

Textsura is an online thrift shop founded by best friends Shen Toca and Keizilk Lamoste, who share a passion for fashion, creativity, and timeless style.

What started as a shared love for discovering unique thrifted treasures grew into a small business dedicated to giving pre-loved clothing a second chance. Every item is carefully selected to ensure quality, individuality, and affordability for every customer.

Based in Legazpi City, Albay, Textsura believes that great style does not have to come with a high price tag. Through sustainable fashion, we encourage people to embrace pre-loved clothing while expressing their own unique personality.

At Textsura, every piece has its own story—waiting to become part of yours.

Mission

  • To provide carefully curated thrifted clothing that is stylish, affordable, and accessible.
  • To promote sustainable fashion by giving pre-loved garments a second life.
  • To inspire confidence, creativity, and individuality through unique fashion finds.

Vision

  • To become a trusted online thrift shop recognized for quality, authenticity, and exceptional customer service.
  • To encourage more people to embrace sustainable and conscious fashion choices.
  • To build a community where style, creativity, and sustainability come together.
